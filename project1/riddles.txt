All the riddles used in our game. Note everytime you go to the Health Sciences building in our game you will be randomly asked one of these 8 riddles.

1.If you multiply this number by any other number, the answer will always be the same. What number is this?
Answer: 0
2.I am an odd number. Take away a letter and I become even. What number am I?
Answer: 7
3.Pronounced as 1 letter, And written with 3, 2 letters there are, and 2 only in me. I’m double, I’m single, I’m black blue, and gray, I’m read from both ends, and the same either way. What am I?
Answer: Eye
4.Pauls height is six feet, he is an assistant at a butchers shop, and wears size 9 shoes. What does he weigh?
Answer: Meat
5.What can go through glass without breaking it?
Answer: Light
6.What is always on its way but never arrives?
Answer: Tomorrow
7.What do you buy to eat but never consume?
Answer: Cutlery
8.Rachel goes to the supermarket and buys 10 tomatoes. Unfortunately, on the way back home, all but 9 get ruined. How many tomatoes are left in a good condition?
Answer: 9
